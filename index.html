8+ Years at the Wheel

The Maker Behind the Clay

Hi, I'm John Ruiz.

Eight years ago I put my hands on a wheel and everything changed. Clay is an ancient teacher — it slows you down, pulls you into the present, and gives something back every time you show up for it.

Spirit Tree Ceramics is two things: handmade pieces fired with intention in my Dallas studio, and online membership circles designed to guide you on your clay journey — providing structured video masterclasses and monthly group Zoom critique circles.

  • 🏺
    One-of-a-Kind Pieces Every pot in the shop is handthrown and glazed individually. Own a piece of the slow craft.
  • 🎥
    Masterclass Library Unlock step-by-step videos. Learn centering, pulling, trimming, and glaze formulation on your schedule.
  • 🔮
    Mentorship & Critique Get direct feedback on your throws. Premium members participate in monthly group Zoom reviews to critique work and progress together.
