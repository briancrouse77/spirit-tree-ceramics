8+ Years at the Wheel

The Keeper of the Wheel

Hi, I'm John Ruiz.

Clay is one of the oldest teachers on earth. It asks you to slow down, to feel, to surrender to the spiral — and in that surrender, something opens. I've spent eight years listening to what it has to say.

Spirit Tree Ceramics is my way of bringing that experience out of the studio and into your world. I pack the wheel, the clay, and everything else into the van and come to you — your backyard, your living room, your event, your life — anywhere in the Dallas–Fort Worth area.
